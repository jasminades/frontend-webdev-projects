About Us

Welcome to the Boxing Club Southpaw, a cornerstone in the world of boxing since its establishment in 1992 in the vibrant city of Sarajevo.
Born out of a passion for the sweet science, our club has stood as a symbol of dedication, discipline, and the pursuit of excellence in the realm of combat sports. Over the years, we have nurtured champions, molded aspiring fighters, and fostered a community that shares the love for the art of boxing.

Our state-of-the-art facilities provide the ideal environment for both beginners and seasoned boxers to refine their skills and reach new heights

. The club's rich history is a testament to our commitment to fostering not just athletes but individuals who embody the values of perseverance, respect, and sportsmanship. At the Boxing Club Southpaw, we don't just train fighters; we cultivate a family where every member plays a crucial role in shaping the legacy of our beloved institution.

Join us in the pursuit of greatness, and let the spirit of the ring guide you on a journey of self-discovery and triumph. Embrace the legacy, feel the camaraderie, and experience the unparalleled thrill of being part of the Ultimate Boxing Club.
